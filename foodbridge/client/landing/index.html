The Crisis

Food Waste is a Global Emergency

Every year, billions of tons of food are wasted while nearly a billion people go to bed hungry. The gap between abundance and need has never been wider.

40%

Of all food produced globally is wasted every year

828M

People worldwide suffer from chronic hunger

$1T

Lost annually due to food waste across global supply chains

8%

Of global greenhouse gas emissions are from food waste

"A restaurant discards 30% of food prepared every single day. Just two blocks away, a family goes to sleep hungry. FoodBridge closes that gap."

This is the problem we solve. Every. Single. Day.

How It Works

The FoodBridge Flow

A seamless pipeline from surplus food to served meals, powered by technology and community.

01

Restaurant Logs Food

Restaurants, hotels, and households quickly log surplus food — photo, quantity, type, and pickup window.

02

Volunteer Accepts Pickup

Nearby volunteers get smart notifications, accept the pickup, and navigate with live map routing.

03

NGO Receives Food

Partner NGOs receive, verify, and log the incoming food — updating their real-time inventory and plans.

04

Meals Are Served

Food reaches families, shelters, and communities. Every donation tracked, verified, and celebrated.
